# Pages

Pages are your search portal to the providers. Each page search one provider, and has different filter options depending 
on the provider. You're not required to use them, and can add content manually by id. But it is a much nicer experience

![pages.png](pages.png)

A provider providers its own filter option, you can pick a nice icon to display it with in your navigation.
