# Connections

To make Mnema integrate more nicely with others it has connections. Allowing you to delegate notifications to discord,
and kick of a series scan in Kavita after a download completes. Native notifications are also managed here as they're implemented
with the same system.

<note>
    You may only have connection per type. And can decide which events to listen to.
</note>
